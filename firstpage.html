Abuja Nigeria

Nigeria, often called the "Giant of Africa," is a vibrant and diverse country located in West Africa. It is the most populous nation on the continent, with over 200 million people representing over 250 ethnic groups and speaking more than 500 languages. Known for its rich cultural heritage, Nigeria is home to iconic landmarks such as Zuma Rock and the bustling city of Lagos. The country is blessed with abundant natural resources, including oil and gas, and has a dynamic economy fueled by agriculture, entertainment, and technology. Despite its challenges, Nigeria remains a land of resilience, creativity, and boundless potential.
